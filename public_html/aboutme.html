A Little About Me

Hi, I'm Clorama Dorvilias
Interaction Designer
(Currently based in London, UK)


  • Born and raised in San José, CA

  • Received my B.S. in Political Science from Santa Clara University in 2009

  • Spent several years developing skills and pursuing my passion in community development working in the Public Sector.

  • I served two AmeriCorps terms, one as a VISTA in Oakland, CA and again in NYC Civic Corps.

  • Served on the Housing & Community Development Advisory Commission for the City of San José

  • Youngest staffmember of team at David Packard's public policy advocacy organization, representing almost 400 tech companies of Silicon Valley, working to advance housing & community development issues

  • Took a solo backpacking trip travelling 10 countries in Europe for 4 months before moving to NYC.

  • Became self-employed as a Graphic Designer and Front-End Developer.

  • Served as an Executive Volunteer for HERA UK in London, helping women who survived trafficking and domestic abuse situations to become successful entrepreneurs

  • **Currently, pursuing my Masters in Interaction Design Communication at University of the Arts: London College of Communication


    • I'm passionate about designing and innovating solutions for people. Problem-solving, creativity, and taking risks for the greater good are my absolute joys in life.
